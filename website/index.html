Why teams try it

It captures the work that normal career tooling misses.

Valor is designed around the reality that modern engineering work already flows through coding agents. Instead of adding another HR system or manual brag doc, it uses that workflow as the coaching surface.

Ambient coaching

Valor shows up in the flow of work itself, so reviews, debugging, and planning become growth moments instead of end-of-week recall.

Evidence over vibes

Valor turns reviews, design work, debugging, and coordination into structured evidence you can reuse for weekly reflection and 1:1 prep.

Bring your own framework

Valor adapts to your company ladder, target level, and growth expectations instead of forcing a generic career rubric.

What It Looks Like

Useful output, not another dashboard.

A good Valor interaction should help you decide what to do next, surface the risk, and connect the work to growth without making you fill out more process.

Carry-forward

Coaching nudge
